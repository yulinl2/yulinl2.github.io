Residual forward-references (5 sites)

Each of these is a \ref{} in the drafts pointing at a target not yet present. They need resolution before outline.tex compiles cleanly (a missing label only yields a "?" in the PDF, not a compile error — but reviewers will catch them).

prop:pessimistic — referenced by theory.tex:98

Context: theory.tex's "Why pessimistic prediction with missing A" paragraph cites Proposition prop:pessimistic in Appendix app:proofs for the EOO-conservativeness of the high-threshold rule.

Resolution: add a Proposition stub to proof_sketch.tex stating: "If A_i is missing, predicting with max(t^0, t^1) instead of t^{A_i} can only decrease TPR for the true (unknown) group, hence cannot increase |DEOO|." The proof is two lines (monotonicity of 1{f ≥ t}).

sec:discussion — referenced by methodology.tex

Lives in Overleaf §9. Either pull the Overleaf discussion into a new _drafts/discussion.tex or change the in-repo cite to limitations.tex which covers the same material.

sec:dr-ablation — referenced by experiments.tex

Could be satisfied by aliasing dr_bootstrap_sketch.tex sec:w2_e2e (which contains the end-to-end DR-ablation empirics) — add \label{sec:dr-ablation} next to the existing \label{sec:w2_e2e}.

sec:shift-ablation — referenced by findings_summary.tex

Could be satisfied by aliasing w_clip_ablation.tex or writing a brief shift-sensitivity ablation subsection from the exp/results/shift_high/ + exp/results/shift_sensitivity/ CSVs.

How to verify after resolution

cd "Fairness with missing/Manuscript/_drafts"
grep -hoP '\\label\{\K[^}]+' *.tex | sort -u > /tmp/labels.txt
grep -hoP '\\(ref|eqref|cref|autoref)\{\K[^}]+' *.tex | sort -u > /tmp/refs.txt
comm -23 /tmp/refs.txt /tmp/labels.txt    # should be empty after fixing
